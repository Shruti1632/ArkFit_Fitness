Body Building

Bodybuilding is a structured form of resistance training focused on enhancing muscular size, symmetry, and definition through progressive overload techniques. Unlike powerlifting, which emphasizes raw strength, bodybuilding emphasizes physique aesthetics, requiring a disciplined approach to training, nutrition, and recovery for optimal physical conditioning and stage-ready results.

Muscle Building

Muscle building focuses on increasing lean muscle mass to improve metabolism, strength, and overall body composition. By engaging in a strategic training program that incorporates resistance exercises, proper nutrition, and recovery, individuals can elevate their energy expenditure, enhance physical performance, and support long-term fat loss goals. Ideal for anyone looking to sculpt a stronger, more defined physique.

Body Mass Index

Whate is BMI?

Body mass index is a value derived from the mass and height of a person. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is expressed in units of kg/m², resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres
